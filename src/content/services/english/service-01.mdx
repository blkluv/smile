# Transforming Dental Practices into Connected Communities

SmileSats revolutionizes patient engagement by creating **private, branded social networks** specifically for your dental practice. ## What is a Patient Social Network?

Think of it as a **private Instagram + Yelp + patient portal** designed exclusively for your practice:

- **Private Community**: Only your verified patients can join and participate
- **Brand-Controlled**: Your practice's branding, your rules, your content
- **Secure Environment**: All patient data and interactions remain private and secure
- **Continuous Engagement**: Keeps patients connected between appointments
